Welcome to Computer Society of India, Delhi Chapter
Computer Society of India (CSI) is the first and largest body of computer professionals in India. It was started on 6 March 1965 by a few computer professionals and has now grown to be the national body representing computer professionals.Computer Society of India (CSI) Delhi Chapter was started on 21 August, 1970 by a few computer professionals.

Historically, Delhi had been the Centre of power from ages. It has been the major activity centre in India both for Business and Industry. In early 1960 itself; Delhi witnessed sufficient growth of computer usage. The population of computer instal lation and computer users has shown exponential increase ev ery successive year. After formation of CSI in 1965, the need was felt to have a activity centre at Delhi. It was on 20th August 1970 when the Constitution & Bye-laws of Delhi Chapter were approved and Chapter got off to a start under the Chairman ship of Prof. J.C. Shourie, IIT Delhi. The chapter has the dis tinction of providing numbers of Presidents to Exe Com. to name few are Dr. R.K. Dutta, Brig. SVS Chowdhry, Dr. M.L. Goyal, R.S. Raizada, Brig. V.M. Sundram, P.P. Gupta.... Chairman's Desk
Dr. Vinay Kumar
Chapter Chairman
I deem it to be a matter of immense pleasure and honour for me to address you all through the website of Computer Society of India, Delhi Chapter. It is indeed very heartening to witness that the CSI, Delhi Chapter has carved a name for itself in the academic and IT professional scenario of the region. Education is the most powerful tool to bring desirable changes in our personality and also to bring positive changes in our society. It is the only medium which enables you to move from darkness to brightness. CSI Student Branches are doing excellent jobs in inculcating such spirits among the students.

Dear friends, I strongly believe that, there can be no better way to drive and improve our nation’s prosperity and social economic well-being than through its IT enabled services. I also believe that, technocrats are the key to continued economic and technological advancement of our country.

I would take this opportunity to urge you all to focus on all round development of the CSI. This task has to be taken over by the academicians to provide value and ethic based training in the society. I earnestly hope and trust that, my esteemed members of the society, aspiring members, academicians and budding technocrats will work with sincerity, honesty and dedication and thereby contribute to make this society in achieving its mission.
-
