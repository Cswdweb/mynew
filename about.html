About Us
Historically, Delhi had been the Centre of power from ages. It has been the major activity centre in India both for Business and Industry. In early 1960 itself; Delhi witnessed sufficient growth of computer usage. The population of computer instal lation and computer users has shown exponential increase ev ery successive year. After formation of CSI in 1965, the need was felt to have a activity centre at Delhi. It was on 20th August 1970 when the Constitution & Bye-laws of Delhi Chapter were approved and Chapter got off to a start under the Chairman ship of Prof. J.C. Shourie, IIT Delhi. The chapter has the dis tinction of providing numbers of Presidents to Exe Com. to name few are Dr. R.K. Dutta, Brig. SVS Chowdhry, Dr. M.L. Goyal, R.S. Raizada, Brig. V.M. Sundram, P.P. Gupta.... The Chapter is one of the largest chapter of the Computer So ciety of India having strength of 1300 members. The various activities carried out by the chapter are :

  • Educational Courses
  • Seminars and Exhibitions
  • Public meetings and Awareness Programmers
  • Conferences and Conventions
  • Evening Technical Talks
  • National Standard Testing Programming Competence
  • Manufacturer's Presentation
  • Panel Discussions
  • Press conference and writing articles in local papers
  • are run by Universities, Board of Technical Examination and
  • Helping to decide curriculum for various courses which
  • Paper competition and Programme competition.
  • internal courses for various public institutes.
  • Teacher's Training Programme
  • Student Branch activities & competition.
  • Promotion of National Activities.
